# de Chaisemartin Reviewer Report: Article 337 — Cameron, Seager, Shah (2021)

**Verdict:** NOT_NEEDED
**Date:** 2026-04-18

## Assessment

### Treatment structure
- Treatment: sex work site closure (closing == 1) enforced Nov 2014.
- Binary and absorbing: once a worksite was closed, it remained closed through the endline.
- Single treatment timing: all treated units receive treatment at the same time (Nov 2014 between wave 1 and wave 2).
- No dose heterogeneity: treatment is 0/1 (open/closed), not a continuous dose.
- No non-absorbing switching: no worksite reopens after closing.

### Conclusion
This is a standard absorbing-binary-single-timing DiD design. The de Chaisemartin & D'Haultfoeuille (2020) estimator is designed for settings with:
- Non-absorbing treatment (switchers and de-adopters), OR
- Continuous/heterogeneous dose at adoption, OR
- Multiple treatment periods with treatment switching.

None of these conditions apply here. The de Chaisemartin estimator is NOT NEEDED for this design.

## Verdict rationale
Clean absorbing-binary-single-timing design. NOT_NEEDED.
